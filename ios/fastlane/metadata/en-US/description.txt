"It's decided, Pon!"

Pon is a simple electronic contract and signature service that lets you create, sign, and manage contracts entirely from your iPhone.

■ Perfect for
• Freelancers and sole proprietors
• Startups and small businesses
• Anyone who regularly needs NDAs, service agreements, and business contracts

■ Key Features

[Contract Creation]
• 5 built-in templates: NDA, Service Agreement, Maintenance, Consulting, Sales
• AI-powered contract generation (first use free)
• Fully editable contract text

[Electronic Signatures]
• Draw your signature with your finger
• Share a URL — the other party signs in their browser
• Two-party signing (Party A and Party B)

[Security]
• SHA-256 document hash for tamper detection
• IP address, timestamp, and User-Agent logged at signing
• Legally valid electronic signatures under Japan's Electronic Signature Act

[Management]
• Track all contracts in one place
• Monthly reports showing contract amounts and counts
• Status tracking: Draft → Sent → Signed → Active

■ About Pon
Basic features are free. The Pro plan unlocks unlimited contracts and AI templates.

■ Security
All communications are encrypted via HTTPS (TLS). Signature data is stored securely on your iPhone.
